How our Modern Pergolas Work

Our modern pergolas have market leading technology that is proving popular across the globe. Each pergola comes with a louvred or PVC sliding roof. The louvres on the roof of our modern pergolas can be rotated and our newest pergola has the feature of completely retracting the louvres to give an open roof. The pergolas also cater for modern day living with integrated or additional ZIP modular screens that keep the cold and the wind outside whenever you want. The option to lower or raise the ZIP screens means the outdoor living area can be altered depending on the temperature and weather. Our pergolas are ideal for a modern garden - they are stylish whilst remaining incredibly practical for all weather.
All the features of our modern pergolas can be controlled by remote control meaning ease of use and complete control of the pergola.

Pergola Customisable Options

  • Sliding sides mean you can choose to turn your pergola into an enclosed area, almost adding a room to your house! Or you can make it into an open area great for parties!
  • Rotatable Louvres allow sun in when you want and keep rain out when you want to stay dry!
  • APP SUPPORT! Control all parts of your pergola with your app and automate it to change whenever and wherever you please!
  • L.E.D. strips can be added into the louvres and can be set to a range of colours!
  • Spotlights within the main structure can also be added to give perfect lighting at night
  • Wind detecting devices can make the pergola automate itself to prevent rain getting in and wind causing damage!
  • Heaters easily attachable to the sides that provide warmth and comfort all year round
  • Advanced draining systems ensure you stay dry and the water is quickly cleared away!

Our pergola has neatly hidden sides that can be raised and lowered at any time to keep in heat or increase the space. When fully raised they are completely hidden, these sides can be made up of any material and colour from our catalogue, including see through materials to act as windows!
The roof of our pergola is made from cleverly designed S-shape louvres which can rotate in all directions in order to get as much sunlight in as possible! They also are easily closed which allows you to still enjoy the extra space and outdoor living even if the weather turns!
Specially developed lights can be modeled to your requirements, with our L.E.D. strips being able to take any colour under the rainbow. These lights are powerful yet subtle, and can be placed anywhere on the pergola: In the louvres, down the legs and on the outside! These verstatile lights make the pergola stand out at night time.
The pergola can stand alone on 4 feet or also be made to lean on your house, requiring only 2 feet! The feet are securely fastened down by our construction team and the pergola has a 5 year warranty as we guarentee it is long lasting. The legs also have in built drainage systems which deal with rainwater and help you stay dry!
