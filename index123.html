Contemporary Pergolas, Awnings and Parasols

Rase Outdoor Living is a supplier and installer of aluminium pergolas, awnings and parasols for your garden. Our products will add to your structure, with our contemporary garden pergola becoming a true focal point. Each individual pergola is made to measure in the Brustor Factory according to customers requirements. The contemporary products have a range of innovative features, our garden pergola contains rotatable louvres and automated sides.
From survey to commissioning we can take care of everything for you.

Bespoke and Contemporary Outdoor Living

Pergolas, Awnings and Parasols are both stylish and functional, as well as adding a focal point to your garden, they can provide shade during the summer as well as shelter and warmth in the winter with their automated fabric sides, and rotating louvres. We work alongside a range of leading companies who specialise in garden living and architecture . With the aid of our aluminium pergolas, cleverly paired with our large range of products, you can create an innovative outdoor design to transform your garden to an area of beauty and functionality. They integrate perfectly with any gardening layouts and add a flooring area that can be left open or enclosed.
