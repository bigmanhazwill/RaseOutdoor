Contemporary Pergolas, Awnings and Parasols

Rase Outdoor Living is a supplier and installer of aluminium pergolas, awnings and parasols for your garden. Our products will add to your structure, with our contemporary garden pergola becoming a true focal point. Each individual pergola is made to measure in the Brustor Factory according to customers requirements. The contemporary products have a range of innovative features, our garden pergola contains rotatable louvres and automated sides.

Explore why our pergola's are trending worldwide:

Our pergola has neatly hidden sides that can be raised and lowered at any time to keep in heat or increase the space. When fully raised they are completely hidden, these sides can be made up of any material and colour from our catalogue, including see through materials to act as windows!
The roof of our pergola is made from cleverly designed S-shape louvres which can rotate in all directions in order to get as much sunlight in as possible! They also are easily closed which allows you to still enjoy the extra space and outdoor living even if the weather turns!
Specially developed lights can be modeled to your requirements, with our L.E.D. strips being able to take any colour under the rainbow. These lights are powerful yet subtle, and can be placed anywhere on the pergola: In the louvres, down the legs and on the outside! These verstatile lights make the pergola stand out at night time.
The pergola can stand alone on 4 feet or also be made to lean on your house, requiring only 2 feet! The feet are securely fastened down by our construction team and the pergola has a 5 year warranty as we guarentee it is long lasting. The legs also have in built drainage systems which deal with rainwater and help you stay dry!
