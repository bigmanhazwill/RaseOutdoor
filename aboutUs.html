Welcome to Rase Outdoor Living website, official installers of Brustor products throughout the North of the UK. Based in Lincolnshire, we are well placed to travel throughout the North to advise about and install all Brustor products. We pride ourselves in customer satisfaction and will work with you throughout the design, purchase and installation process to ensure it. We will happily visit your premises at your convenience to discuss product options and show you product samples. Also, throughout 2016 we will be displaying Brustor products at all the major County shows should you wish to experience them first hand prior to purchase. Brustor products are very versatile - they provide cover and warmth in the cooler spring and autumn seasons, as well as offering excellent shade and comfort on sunny days, roof and sides close to retain warmth and afford shelter.
